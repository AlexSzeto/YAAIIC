# AnyTale Sound Effects

**Priority:** medium

## Goal

Add seamlessly looping sound effects to AnyTale that trigger on specific plot pages. Sound effects can be generated dynamically or pulled from a fixed library.

## Notes

- Needs a way to associate sound effects with specific plot page types or tags.
- Seamless looping is a hard requirement — gapless audio playback.
- Open question: generated vs. library — may support both modes.
