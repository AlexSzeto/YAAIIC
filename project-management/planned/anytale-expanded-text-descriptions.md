# AnyTale Expanded Text Descriptions

**Priority:** medium

## Goal

Increase the amount of user-facing textual content throughout AnyTale — including tooltips, labels, helper text, and descriptive copy — so users have a clearer understanding of what each field, control, and section does without needing prior knowledge of the system.

## Notes

- Applies to both the editor (`/anytale-editor.html`) and play mode (`/anytale.html`).
- Could include: field labels with explanatory subtext, empty-state messaging, section descriptions, tooltip hints on ambiguous controls.
- Should not clutter the UI — descriptions should be concise and toggleable or tucked behind info icons where appropriate.
